Namespace CopperSharp.Advancements.Triggers

Classesâ€‹

BeeNestDestroyedâ€‹

Bee nest destroyed trigger condition

BredAnimalsâ€‹

Triggers after the player breeds 2 animals.

BrewedPotionâ€‹

Triggers after the player takes any item out of a brewing stand.

ChangedDimensionâ€‹

Triggers after the player travels between two dimensions

ChanneledLightningâ€‹

Triggers after the player successfully uses the Channeling enchantment on an entity or a lightning rod

ConstructBeaconâ€‹

Triggers after the player changes the structure of a beacon. (When the beacon updates itself).

ConsumeItemâ€‹

Triggers when the player consumes an item

CuredZombieVillagerâ€‹

Triggers when the player cures a zombie villager

EffectChangedâ€‹

Triggers after the player gets a status effect applied or taken from them

EnchantedItemâ€‹

Triggers after the player enchants an item through an enchanting table (does not get triggered through an anvil, or through commands).

EnterBlockâ€‹

Triggers when the player stands in a block. Checks every tick and will try to trigger for each successful match (up to 8 times, the maximum amount of blocks a player can stand in), which only works if the advancement is revoked from within the advancement using a function reward

EntityDamagePlayerâ€‹

Triggers after a player gets hurt, even without a source entity (idk why it is named like that, ask mojang)

EntityKillPlayerâ€‹

Triggers after a living entity kills a player

EntityPickUpItemâ€‹

Triggers after the player throws an item and another entity picks it up.

FallFromHeightâ€‹

Triggers when a player lands after falling

FilledBucketâ€‹

Triggers after the player fills a bucket

FishingRodHookedâ€‹

Triggers after the player successfully catches an item with a fishing rod or pulls an entity with a fishing rod

HeroOfTheVillageâ€‹

Triggers when the player defeats a raid.

InventoryChangedâ€‹

Triggers after any changes happen to the player's inventory

ItemDurabilityChangedâ€‹

Triggers after any item in the inventory has been damaged in any form.

ItemUsedOnBlockâ€‹

Triggers when the player uses their hand or an item on a block

KilledByCrossbowâ€‹

Triggers after the player kills a mob or player using a crossbow in ranged combat

LevitationTriggerâ€‹

Triggers when the player has the levitation status effect

LightningStrikeTriggerâ€‹

Triggers when a lightning bolt disappears from the world, only for players within a 256 block radius of the lightning bolt

LocationTriggerâ€‹

Triggers every 20 ticks (1 second) and checks where the player is

NetherTravelâ€‹

Triggers when the player travels to the Nether and then <strong>returns</strong> to the Overworld

NullTriggerâ€‹

Never triggers

PlacedBlockâ€‹

Triggers when the player places a block.

PlayerDamageEntityâ€‹

Triggers after the player hurts a mob or player.

PlayerGenerateLootâ€‹

Triggers when the player generates the contents of a container with a loot table set. E.g. kills an entity or opens a dungeon chest.

PlayerInteractEntityâ€‹

Triggers when the player interacts with an entity.

PlayerKillEntityâ€‹

Triggers after a player is the source of a mob or player being killed.

RecipeUnlockedâ€‹

Triggers after the player unlocks a recipe (e.g. using a knowledge book).

ShotCrossbowâ€‹

Triggers when the player shoots a crossbow.

SleptInBedâ€‹

Triggers when the player enters a bed.

SlideDownBlockâ€‹

Triggers when the player slides down a block.

StartedRidingâ€‹

Triggers when the player starts riding a vehicle or an entity starts riding a vehicle currently ridden by the player. Note, that all the vehicle data must be stored using the CopperSharp.Advancements.TriggerCondition.RequirePlayer(CopperSharp.Advancements.Predicates.EntityPredicate)

SummonedEntityâ€‹

Triggers after an entity has been summoned. Works with iron golems (pumpkin and iron blocks), snow golems (pumpkin and snow blocks), the ender dragon (end crystals) and the wither (wither skulls and soul sand/soul soil). Using dispensers to place the wither skulls or pumpkins will still activate this trigger. Spawn eggs, commands and mob spawners will not work however.

TameAnimalâ€‹

Triggers after the player tames an animal.

TargetHitâ€‹

Triggers when the player shoots a target block

TickTriggerâ€‹

Triggers every tick (~20 times a second).

UsedEnderEyeâ€‹

Triggers when the player uses an eye of ender (in a world where strongholds generate).

UsedTotemâ€‹

Triggers when the player uses a totem.

UsingItemâ€‹

Triggers for every tick that the player uses an item that is used continuously. It is known to trigger for bows, crossbows, honey bottles, milk buckets, potions, shields, spyglasses, tridents, food items and eyes of ender. Most items that activate from a single click, such as fishing rods, do not affect this trigger.

VillagerTradeCompleteâ€‹

Triggers after the player trades with a villager or a wandering trader.

VoluntaryExileTriggerâ€‹

Triggers when the player causes a raid.

Enumsâ€‹

NamedPotionâ€‹

Represents a type of potion name. Not to be confused with CopperSharp.Data.Effects.StatusEffect
